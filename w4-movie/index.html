About Cars

Cars is a movie where every character is a vehicle rather than a normal person. Based around the main protagonist, Lightning McQueen, he finds himself in a little town called Radiator Springs after losing contact with Mack, his escort to races. He aspires to win a piston cup, the reward for winning the prestigious race he's always dreamed of.
