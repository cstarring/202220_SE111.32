Cars

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

REVIEW

Chris Starring February 8 2022

Cars is a great movie in my opinion, because it tells a fun tale of a race car that goes on to become something bigger than he ever thought he would be. I've always really liked cars, so I may be slightly biased when it comes to the reason I enjoyed it. As a kid I always wanted to be like McQueen because he was fast, and because of his catchphrase, "I am speed". When I was around 7 or so I'd run around the house pretending to be a race car.


My favorite scene from the movie is easily when the four tuner cars come across Mack, falling asleep on the highway. I always loved how cool the intro scene looked. Snot rod was my favorite character when i was younger, but now that I actually know more about how cars work, what types there are, etc. I'd probably say it's boost.


To add to the statement above, the animators went out of their way to model them after real-world cars, which I think is a nice touch. For example, Snot Rod is based off of a 1970s Dodge Challenger. Boost is a Nissan 240SX/200SX.

Trivia
  • Did you know that The King was actually voiced by THE Richard Petty?
  • While some may think DJ is based off a Subaru Forester or Kia Soul, he's actually a Scion xB.
  • Did you know McQueen was based off a Chevrolet Corvette?
